## Why tokens matter

When colors are hardcoded into individual components, every redesign becomes expensive. A token-based system keeps your interface flexible and much easier to maintain.

## Better for iteration

Changing `--background`, `--card`, `--border`, and `--muted-foreground` at the system level lets the whole site move together.

## Practical payoff

- Faster redesigns
- More consistent surfaces
- Easier dark mode support
- Fewer styling regressions

## The real win

A theme system is not just about design consistency. It also reduces decision fatigue while building.
